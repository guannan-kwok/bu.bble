Frequently Asked Questions

bu.bble is a mental health platform where users can anonymously share their frustrations and personal thoughts or feelings. Users can also anonymously seek help from mental health care professionals and find activities to help them reliev stress.

bu.bble is currently supported on all IOS and Android devices. A desktop version will be available November 2021.

bu.bble lets you add hashtags to better categorize your posts. You can also choose to post publically or privately.

Public post appear at random on other users feeds.

Private posts appear in your diary and are only viewable in this section.

bu.bble allows you to delete public and private posts at any time. bu.bble allows you to wipe all personal data if uninstalling the application.

bu.bble allows you to schedule a one-on-one meeting with a mental health professional. You can do this via phone call, by messaging or meet up in person at the health facility.

bu.bble also provided curated resources to relieve stress and anxiety.
